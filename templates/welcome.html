Good health means a balance of physical activity, nutritious food, mental well-being, and positive relationships.

Tip: A balanced diet includes a variety of foods from all food groups: fruits, vegetables, grains, protein, and dairy.

Health Tips

  • Drink plenty of water every day.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy body.
  • Get enough sleep each night.
  • Practice good hygiene and wash your hands often.
  • Take breaks and manage stress effectively.
  • Visit your doctor for regular check-ups.
